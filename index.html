Every feature was designed with one person in mind — the independent contractor who wears every hat and needs tools that actually work on a job site.

JOB

Job Manager

Full pipeline: Estimate → Scheduled → In Progress → Complete → Invoiced → Paid. Job cards with client info, address, trade type, photos, and notes. Search and filter every job in one place.

AI Auto-Fill
PRF

Profit Calculator

True net profit per job. Labor, materials, subcontractor fees, overhead %, travel cost, and permit fees all factored in. Know your real margin before you start the job.

EST

Estimate Builder

Professional line-item estimates with labor and materials broken out. Client approval link — they sign from their phone. One-click convert to scheduled job. Version history on revisions.

Digital Signature
INV

Invoice Generator

PDF invoices with your company name and license number. Partial payment tracking. Draft → Sent → Viewed → Paid status. Overdue alerts with late fee language built in.

CAL

Job Calendar

Monthly and weekly view color-coded by trade type. Travel time buffers between jobs. Weather check per job day. Prevent double booking. Tap any day to add a job.

Weather-Aware
MAT

Materials Tracker

Log materials per job by item, quantity, supplier, and cost. Running total auto-deducted from profit. Monthly spend by category. Flag frequent materials as favorites.

SUP

Supplier Price Tracker

Log what you paid for the same material at different suppliers over time. CSO Field builds a price history so you always know who's cheaper this month. Nobody else does this.

Exclusive Feature
DOC

Client Manager

Full client address book with complete job history, lifetime value, property notes, and preferred contact method. One-click new job from any client profile.

PHO

Job Photo Log

Before, during, and after photos tagged to each job phase. Timestamped. Export as PDF for warranty documentation or insurance claims. Optional watermark with your company name.

JOB

Digital Inspection Checklist

Customizable pre-job and post-job checklists per trade type. Client gets a signed PDF on completion. Competitors charge extra for this — it's built into every plan here.

Exclusive Feature
CHG

Change Order Manager

Scope changed on-site? Create a change order from your phone, update the price, and get client digital approval before continuing. Eliminates "I never agreed to that" disputes.

Better Than Competitors
TIME

On-Site Time Clock

Clock in on arrival, clock out on departure. Tracks actual vs estimated hours per job. Crew members clock in independently. Shows which job types run over estimate so you can reprice.

OVH

Overhead Analyzer

Input your monthly fixed costs — truck, insurance, fuel, tools, phone. CSO Field calculates your true cost per hour and minimum job rate to break even. Flags underpriced jobs automatically.

Exclusive Feature
RPTS

Job Type Profitability

"Your bathroom remodels average $847 net. Your drywall patches average $92." Know exactly which job types are worth taking. Competitors show revenue — we show what you actually keep.

Exclusive Feature
RTE

Route Optimizer

Running 3 jobs today? AI suggests the most fuel-efficient order based on addresses. Built in — not an add-on. Saves 30-60 minutes and real fuel money every day.

Free — No Add-On
REV

Review Requester

Auto-generates a Google review request after job completion. Send via text or email. Track which clients left reviews. Contractors live and die by reviews — this alone pays for the subscription.

REC

Maintenance Plan Manager

Create recurring service agreements — client pays monthly or yearly, jobs auto-schedule. HVAC, pest control, landscaping. A huge revenue upgrade most affordable tools completely ignore.

FLW

Automated Follow-Ups

Estimate not responded to in 48 hours? Auto follow-up. Job complete? Auto thank-you + review request. 6 months later? Auto check-in. Contractors leave thousands on the table from dropped leads.

MSG

Client Communication Log

Every call note, text, and message logged per job. Never lose track of what was promised. Full paper trail when clients dispute scope or pricing.

LIC

License & Insurance Tracker

Input license, insurance, bond, and vehicle registration expiry dates. Alerts at 60, 30, and 7 days out. Getting caught with an expired license on a job site is a serious problem.

Exclusive Feature

Crew Manager
